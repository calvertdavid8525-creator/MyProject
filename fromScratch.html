The Art of 3-Pointers





How to Shoot

  1. Set your feet square to the hoop.
  2. Rest the ball on the palm of your dominate hand with your off hand to the side.
  3. Hold the ball about eye-level.
  4. Focus your eyes on the back of the rim.
  5. Raise the ball up and flick your wrist, shooting it toward the hoop.
  6. Give the ball good arc and jump while you shoot it.
